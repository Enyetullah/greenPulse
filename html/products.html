Disease Analyzer:

The Disease Analyzer in GreenPulse is designed to help farmers proactively protect their crops. By using advanced scanning and diagnostic tools, this feature identifies potential disease risks early on, analyzing symptoms and environmental factors to pinpoint vulnerabilities. With tailored recommendations for treatment and prevention, the Disease Analyzer empowers farmers to safeguard their crops, ensuring healthier yields and reducing the need for reactive measures.

Map Detector:

GreenPulse is an innovative app designed to enhance agricultural awareness by providing users with an interactive map of farmland plots. Each plot is marked on the map, and when users click on a specific area, they can access detailed information about the crops being cultivated. This feature not only helps users understand the diversity of agricultural practices in their region but also fosters a connection between consumers and local farming efforts. With GreenPulse, exploring the world of agriculture becomes an engaging and educational experience.

Search:

The search feature in GreenPulse empowers users to quickly find detailed information about specific crops by simply entering their names into a search bar. This functionality streamlines the process of discovering essential data such as growth conditions, nutritional information, and pest management strategies for various crops. Users can gain valuable insights into the characteristics and requirements of the crops they are interested in, whether for personal gardening, academic research, or professional farming. By making crop information readily accessible, GreenPulse enhances the user experience and encourages informed decision-making in agricultural practices.
